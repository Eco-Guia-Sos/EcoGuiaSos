# EcoGuía SOS: Project Brief

## Core Mission
EcoGuía SOS is a community-driven platform designed to connect volunteers and altruistic citizens with environmental, recycling, and protection events in Mexico City (CDMX). It serves as a central hub (the "Community Bridge") to organize and validate disparate information from social media into a structured, reliable, and accessible guide.

## Problem Statement
Environmental and altruistic events in CDMX are often scattered across Instagram, Facebook, and WhatsApp groups. Information is easily lost, location clarity is poor, and there is a lack of a central "trust" authority to verify the legitimacy of new organizations.

## Goals
1.  **Reliability**: Provide validated event information through an Admin-Actor verification system.
2.  **Engagement**: Use gamification (EcoOficial badges) and user-centric features to encourage altruism.
3.  **Accessibility**: Maintain a public-first browsing experience with optional registration for advanced features.
4.  **Community Growth**: Enable organizations (Actors) to easily manage, clone, and promote recurring events.

## Key Outcomes
*   A live, interactive map of CDMX events.
*   A tiered permission system (Super Admin, Verified Actor, Public User).
*   A "Contextual UI" that allows organizations to manage content directly on the page.

## Behavioral Rules: The "Opponent" Protocol
*   **Destruir con datos**: Debo desafiar las solicitudes del usuario si atentan contra la estabilidad, el minimalismo técnico o la coherencia estética (Vanilla CSS/Bento Grid).
*   **Eficiencia de Tokens**: Rechazar integraciones masivas de herramientas que diluyan la memoria operativa.
